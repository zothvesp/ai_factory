# Goal Template

## Purpose

The goal template provides a reusable structure for starting AI-assisted work.

## Template

```markdown
# Goal Record

## Goal Statement

What must be achieved, for whom, and why.

## Problem or Opportunity

Current pain, risk, or value opportunity.

## Stakeholders

Roles affected and authority for decisions.

## Scope

Included work.

## Non-Scope

Explicitly excluded work.

## Assumptions

Unverified statements accepted temporarily.

## Constraints

Binding limits and verification methods.

## Risks

Likelihood, impact, mitigation, owner, and trigger.

## Acceptance Criteria

Observable conditions that prove the goal.

## Exit Criteria

Evidence required to stop.

## Success Metrics

KPIs or phase measures.

## Required Roles and Loops

AI roles and engineering loops needed.
```

## Bad Example

```text
Goal: Improve docs.
```

## Good Example

```text
Goal: Complete Goal Engineering support pages so future agents can define
assumptions, constraints, risks, KPIs, exit criteria, and goal records without
using skeletal templates.
```

## AI Guidance

- Fill the template before substantial work.
- Keep non-scope explicit.
- Mark assumptions and risks separately.
- Update the template if recurring work needs additional evidence.

## Review Checklist

- Goal is specific and valuable.
- Scope and non-scope are clear.
- Criteria and metrics are observable.
- Roles and loops are named.
- Risks and assumptions are visible.
